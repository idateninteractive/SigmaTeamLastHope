ABOUT THIS GAME

The year is 2161 and aliens are once again trying to invade Earth. You are a mercenary from the M.A.G.M.A Corporation who is sent to help the United States Army get rid of the plague. So, under the command of General Baker, you take on missions and kill many thousands of aliens in your path.
